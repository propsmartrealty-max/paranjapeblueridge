**Paranjape Schemes Redefines the Hinjewadi Skyline with the Completion of Ultra-Luxury Altius Towers**

**PUNE, MAHARASHTRA** — Paranjape Schemes (Construction) Ltd., a highly respected name in the <a href="https://www.paranjapeblueridge.com">Pune Real Estate Market</a>, has officially announced the completion of the structural framework for The Altius — its most premium offering within the award-winning <a href="https://www.paranjapeblueridge.com/paranjape-blue-ridge-altius-hinjewadi-pune">Paranjape Blue Ridge Township Hinjewadi</a>.

The Altius features sprawling 4 and 5 BHK residences, offering panoramic views of the adjacent 9-hole golf course and the Mula River. The project caters specifically to the C-suite executives and IT leadership stationed in Hinjewadi Phase 1, offering a zero-commute luxury lifestyle.

"With The Altius, we are delivering an unparalleled living experience that rivals the finest global townships. The demand for ultra-luxury residences in Hinjewadi has surged, and <a href="https://www.paranjapeblueridge.com">Paranjape Blue Ridge</a> stands as the definitive answer," said a spokesperson for Paranjape Schemes.

The 138-acre integrated township already boasts an operational ICSE school, a private boat club, and a vibrant community of over 5,000 families. The addition of The Altius solidifies Blue Ridge as the premier luxury destination in West Pune.
