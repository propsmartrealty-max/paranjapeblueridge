**Paranjape Schemes Redefines the Hinjewadi Skyline with 'Ridges 41', the Latest Addition to the 138-Acre Blue Ridge Township**

**PUNE, INDIA – [Date]** – Paranjape Schemes Construction Ltd. (PSCL), one of Western India's most trusted real estate developers, has announced the launch of 'Ridges 41', a premium residential cluster located within the award-winning Paranjape Blue Ridge Township in Hinjewadi Phase 1. Designed to cater specifically to IT professionals and modern families, Ridges 41 introduces smart 2 BHK and 3 BHK configurations built with cutting-edge Mivan technology.

As Pune’s IT sector continues its rapid expansion, Hinjewadi Phase 1 has evolved into the city's premier corporate hub. Paranjape Blue Ridge stands alone as the only fully operational, 138-acre integrated township in the area, offering a true "walk-to-work" ecosystem. With the introduction of Ridges 41, the developers are bringing a fresh wave of premium inventory to a micro-market that is currently facing a massive supply deficit.

"Blue Ridge has always been more than just a residential project; it is a meticulously planned city within a city," stated a spokesperson for Paranjape Schemes. "With the upcoming Pune Metro Line 3 station located just 800 meters from our gates, residents of Ridges 41 and our luxury clusters like [Promenade Residences](https://www.paranjapeblueridge.com/paranjape-blue-ridge-promenade-hinjewadi-pune) will enjoy unprecedented connectivity to Baner, Balewadi, and Shivajinagar."

**A Complete Ecosystem with Zero Commute**
Unlike standalone building projects in Pune West, the [Paranjape Blue Ridge Township Hinjewadi](https://www.paranjapeblueridge.com) ecosystem includes:
- **The Blue Ridge SEZ:** A massive corporate park inside the township gates housing global IT giants.
- **Blue Ridge Public School:** An operational, highly-rated ICSE school ensuring children walk safely to class.
- **Luxury Amenities:** A 9-hole golf course, a private riverfront boat club, high-street retail, and multiple polyclinics.

Real estate analysts predict that the launch of Ridges 41 will drive significant interest from both end-users and investors. The township has historically generated rental yields exceeding 4-5%—the highest in Pune—driven by the continuous influx of IT expats demanding premium, secure, and amenity-rich housing near their offices.
